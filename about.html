About

About us

American Institute of Telepsychiatry

The American Institute of Telepsychiatry is a Telepsychiatry Clinic committed to providing quality online psychiatry with comfort, convenience, and privacy from your own home. Our services provide the benefits of convenient and confidential mental health care at an affordable price via the online delivery of health care services and clinical information. Telepsychiatry is one of the most promising developments to provide more patient-centered, affordable, and effective interventions for individuals who need psychiatric care. Our providers are committed to providing the best quality care to each and every one of our patients.

At The American Institute of Telepsychiatry, our providers are experts in clinical advice with the latest psychotropic medication regime recommendations and guidelines for a wide variety of psychiatric conditions needing management. Our panel of clinicians are specially trained in the latest evidence-based guidelines and approaches to psychiatric phenomenology. Using telepsychiatry, psychiatrists and other mental health providers will directly interact with the patient via the telephone or video conferencing platforms.

Why Choose Telepsychiatry?

Convenience and Comfort

Telepsychiatry is suitable for people of all ages, including children, adolescents, and adults. Telepsychiatry has proven to help reduce or eliminate:

  • Travel time and potential travel barriers
  • Lower patient costs (gas, hiring a sitter, etc.)
  • Delays in psychiatric care or mental health treatment
  • The need for trips to the emergency room
  • The need for time off work or school
  • The barrier of stigma
  • The incidents of relapse and decompensation

Why Choose Telepsychiatry?

Benefits and Improvements

Telepsychiatry has proven to increase and improve:

  • Privacy and Compliance
  • Continuity of psychiatric care and mental health treatment
  • Medical access for people in underserved areas
  • Medical access for people without health insurance
  • Patient Satisfaction
  • Comfort and convenience
  • Cost savings and accessibility

Why Choose Telepsychiatry?

Efficient High-Quality Care

Benefits of Virtual Psychiatry:

  • Provides better access to mental health treatment
  • Allows for faster, more cost-effective treatment
  • Addresses the shortage of qualified mental health professionals
  • Enables the expansion of value-based services
  • Offers patient convenience
  • Reduces staffing costs
  • Increases the volume of patients that physicians can reach and treat
  • Gives providers flexibility
